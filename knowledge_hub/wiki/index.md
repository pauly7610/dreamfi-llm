# DreamFi Product Knowledge Hub

## How to Use This Wiki

This wiki is the single source of truth for DreamFi product knowledge.
It is maintained by an LLM-powered pipeline that ingests information
from Confluence, Jira, Slack exports, Figma, and manual notes, then
organizes it into structured, cross-referenced pages.

**For humans:**
- Browse the categories below to find what you need.
- Use `python -m knowledge_hub.query "your question"` to ask natural
  language questions grounded in this wiki.
- Every page has source citations -- follow them for deeper context.

**For the LLM pipeline:**
- All pages follow the schema defined in `schema/CLAUDE.md`.
- The ingestion engine (`ingest.py`) creates and updates pages.
- The lint tool (`lint.py`) enforces quality and freshness.
- This index is auto-maintained -- do not edit it manually.
